The Mission

We're rebuilding the infrastructure that powers every tech team.

Right now, hiring a software engineer takes an average of 47 days and costs companies over $28,000 — in agency fees, recruiter hours, and lost productivity. The tools companies rely on were built for a keyword-based world, not an AI-native one. Résumés get filtered by the wrong signals. Great candidates never surface. Recruiters spend 80% of their time on admin, not decisions.

ELINSI is a single intelligent engine that handles the entire hiring pipeline: it reads job descriptions, understands context, ranks candidates by real fit, automates screening, and delivers one-page decision reports — in under 48 hours. No keyword filters. No manual screens. Just the right person, faster.

And unlike any tool before it, ELINSI gets smarter with every hire. The Hiring Brain learns from every outcome — so the 50th hire is dramatically more accurate than the first. That's a compounding advantage that can't be replicated by bolting AI onto a legacy ATS.

How It Works

From job post to finalist in 4 steps.

01
Post a Role
Paste any job description. ELINSI extracts skills, seniority signals, and culture requirements in 2 seconds.
02
AI Matching
The Hiring Brain ranks candidates by contextual fit — not keywords — producing a shortlist in under 15 minutes.
03
Auto Screening
Role-specific questions sent, collected, and scored with zero recruiter involvement required.
04
Decision Report
One-page finalist profiles with scores, screening answers, and a clear hire/pass recommendation.
