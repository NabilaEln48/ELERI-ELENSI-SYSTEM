What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled. Examples: session tokens, CSRF protection, authentication state.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how users interact with the platform (page views, feature usage). We use privacy-preserving analytics with no cross-site tracking.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings such as language, plan toggle state, and display preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies — Only set if you opt in. Used to measure effectiveness of outreach campaigns. No data is shared with ad networks.

Third-Party Cookies

We use Stripe for payment processing, which sets its own cookies governed by Stripe's privacy policy. We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, or other advertising trackers on the core platform.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will affect platform functionality. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies in Settings → Privacy or Security.

Cookie Retention

  • Session cookies — expire when you close your browser
  • Authentication tokens — expire after 30 days of inactivity
  • Analytics cookies — expire after 12 months
  • Preference cookies — expire after 12 months

Getting Started

Click "Get Started Free" on the homepage. Enter your email and create a password. Select whether you're a Candidate or Company. Complete your profile — the more detail you add, the better your matches. Verification email will arrive within 2 minutes.
Candidate accounts let you upload your resume, track applications, and be discovered by hiring companies. Company accounts let you post jobs, view ranked shortlists, run automated screenings, and receive pre-screen reports. You cannot switch between types after signup — create a new account if needed.
ELINSI's AI analyzes job descriptions to extract required skills, seniority levels, experience signals, and cultural cues. It then scores each candidate in the pool across multiple dimensions — technical fit, experience level, trajectory, and soft-skill signals from screening answers. Every score is explainable: you can see exactly why a candidate ranked where they did.
After posting a job, your initial shortlist is typically ready within 15 minutes. If your requirements are very specific or niche, we may take up to 2 hours to ensure quality matches. You'll receive an email notification when your shortlist is ready.
Once you approve a shortlist, ELINSI sends role-specific screening questions to your top candidates automatically. Candidates complete async video or text responses. The AI scores and summarizes their answers, and delivers a pre-screen report for each finalist — no recruiter involvement needed until you're ready to interview.
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. You can downgrade or cancel anytime. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. Downgrading from Pro to Free will limit future features but won't delete your data. Refunds are not available for partial billing periods.
ELINSI is designed to support — not replace — human hiring decisions. Our AI does not use protected characteristics (race, gender, age, religion, disability) as scoring factors. We recommend all employers conduct their own compliance review for their jurisdiction. Enterprise customers can request a compliance guide from our team.
Use the Contact page to send us a message. Growth and Enterprise customers receive priority support with a 4-hour SLA on business days. Free and Candidate users are supported via email with a 48-hour response target. Live chat is available for Growth and Enterprise plans.
