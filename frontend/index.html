Core Capabilities

Four engines.
One seamless flow.

Resume Parsing

Drop any resume format — PDF, DOCX, plain text. ELINSI extracts every relevant data point: experience, skills, education, certifications. Zero manual entry. Batch upload hundreds at once.

Candidate Scoring

Every candidate gets a multi-dimensional score — role fit, experience depth, skill coverage, career trajectory. Not keyword matching. Actual evaluation of who this person is and where they're going.

Smart Ranking

Candidates ranked strongest to weakest, automatically. The engine learns from every hire you make — which profiles you flagged, which you selected — and gets sharper with each session.

Report Generation

One-page PDF per candidate. Score breakdown, career signals, skill map, contact details — everything your team needs to make a decision without a second meeting.
